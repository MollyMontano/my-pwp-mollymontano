My PWP Project

Pecan River Provisions Website

PURPOSE: This a professional website designed to showcase this new restaurant and drive business.

AUDIENCE: We want to drive locals to the site. Travellers to Albuquerque. Local and faraway
foodies looking for inspired food.

GOAL: The goal is to showcase this new restaurant concept to the world.
In order to drive business and buzz by the time the restaurant opens
in Spring of 2021.

PERSONA:

NAME: Michelle Park

Age:37

Profession: Outside Sales Representative for a Zuna Corporation, a commercial printer.
She has been there for 5 years and they love her. She has doubled their business.
She spends her days our visiting her customers in and around Albuquerque.

Family Lives with her husband. They moved here 7 years ago from Southern California.
They have a 5 year old daughter. The rest of their families are out of state.

Technology: Mac power user. Uses a MacBook Air in Rose gold and a iPhone 11 with unlimited
data connection from Verizon (her husband Andy works there). She is a social

They eat out together a couple times a week. She eats out with her husband at least once a week

Frustrations and Needs: She wants to go to restaurant website, get a feel
for what they are about. She wants to see a menu with pricing. Address, phone number,

Goals: Michelle's goal is to enjoy the restaurant experience and then share with her
friends what cool new places she has found.

User Story: As a frequent user, Michelle wants to get a feel for the restaurant, food, style,
pricing, hours and location easily.
