My PWP Project

Pecan River Provisions Website

Content Strategy This site will be a one page site for an upcoming business and will consist of the following sections.

NAVIGATION BAR This will have the business name with a logo and links to go directly to the sections below: Our Story, The food, Gallery and Contact us.

WELCOME This will contain the mission statement for the business along with a banner picture that captures the feel of the business.

OUR STORY A short paragraph describing the inspiration for the restaurant. With a brief history of chef's experience. It will highlight that chef is from Albuquerque, then left and cooked professionally in Sante Fe, San Francisco and is now back to showcase his offerings in his hometown. This will showcase his commitment to the culinary arts and his love of giving back to the local community. There will be a picture of chef.

THE FOOD This section will describe the different types of food offerings. This will describe the sandwiches, bowls, to go meals as well as the daily baked good offerings. Catering options available.

GALLERY Pictures of chef's food.

CONTACT US

Here will be a way to contact the business for employment or catering opportunities.

FOOTER This will contain the business address an links to social media.
