My PWP Project

Pecan River Provisions Website

Content Strategy

This will be a one page site for an upcoming business and will consist of the following sections.

NAVIGATION BAR

This will have the business name with a logo and links to go directly to the sections below:
The food, Gallery and Contact us.

WELCOME

This will contain the mission statement for the business along with a banner picture that
captures the feel of the business.

OUR STORY

A short paragraph describing the inspiration for the restaurant. With a brief history of chef's
experience. It will highlight that chef is from Albuquerque, then left and cooked professionally
in Sante Fe, San Francisco and is now back to showcase his offerings in his hometown. This will
showcase his commitment to the culinary arts and his love of giving back to the local community.
There will be a picture of chef.

THE FOOD

This section will describe the different types of food offerings. Eat-in, togo, dinners ready to
go. We will talk about the rotisserie and daily baked goods as well as catering options available.

GALLERY

Pictures of chef's food.

CONTACT US

Here will be a way to contact the business for employment or catering opportunities.

FOOTER This will contain the business address an links to social media.
