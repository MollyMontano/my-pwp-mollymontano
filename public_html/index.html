About Us




Pecan River Provisions is a new concept from husband and wife team Javier and Molly Montano. They create fun everyday meals. Centered around the rotisserie where the Pecan Coffee Chile rubbed chicken will be the star. There will be seasonal fresh salads and rotating sides. There will be daily assortment of desserts.

Chef Javier has been cooking for 30 plus years here in his homestate of New Mexico and 15 years in California. At Pecan River Provisions chef wants to bring you into his kitchen and cook for you how he likes to eat. He is influened by his love of travel and exploration sourcing ingredients from Asia, South America and Europe.
