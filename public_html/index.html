About Us

Pecan River Provisions is a new concept from husband and wife team Javier and Molly Montano. The kitchen will be the heart of the space with the rotisserie the star. The coffee pecan chile rubed chicken with scallion mojo will be the signature dish. There will be seasonal desserts, vegetables and salads. Working with local farmers and purveyors as much as possible.

Chef Javier has been cooking for 30 plus years here in his homestate of New Mexico and 15 years in California. At Pecan River Provisions chef wants to bring you into his kitchen and cook for you how he likes to eat. He is influened by his love of travel and exploration adding flavors from Asia, South America and Europe to classic American favourites.
